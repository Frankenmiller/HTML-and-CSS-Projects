Photo of very Handsome Cheweenie Dog

Very naughty breed of hybrid dog but also very possessive of their handlers, they will attack almost anything that threatens them, even if it is much larger than them.

They are very sweet companions and love to be held. If you treat them as your child they will respond very well, loving you for your entire life. Some dog experts recommend that you do not sleep with your dogs, but I don't know how one could resist, or really what was the point of having a dog if it wasn't going to accompany you while resting.
